Clothing Lending App

Partnered with a Scrum team of 5 to develop a Cataloging & Lending app for clothing items. Users can upload items to loan, request to borrow items, create collections, and specify user permissions.
Implemented with Django backend, PostgreSQL database, and deployed on Heroku.
Applied critical thinking and collaborative problem-solving to devise solutions in a simulated SWE team.
